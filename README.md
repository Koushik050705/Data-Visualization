# Data Visualization

## Objective

The objective of this task was to create visual representations of business data to identify trends and communicate insights effectively.

## Tools Used

- Python
- Jupyter Notebook
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn

## Visualizations Created

### Revenue by Product

Visualized revenue contribution of each product.

### Product Order Distribution

Compared product popularity based on order count.

### Payment Method Analysis

Analyzed customer payment preferences.

### Order Status Distribution

Visualized delivered, cancelled, and returned orders.

### Referral Source Analysis

Evaluated customer acquisition channels.

### Quantity Distribution

Studied purchasing behavior patterns.

### Monthly Revenue Trend

Analyzed revenue growth over time.

### Correlation Heatmap

Examined relationships among numerical variables.

## Key Insights

- Identified top-performing products.
- Evaluated marketing channel effectiveness.
- Discovered customer payment preferences.
- Observed monthly sales trends.

## Outcome

Visualizations helped transform raw data into meaningful insights that support business decision-making.# Data-Visualization
